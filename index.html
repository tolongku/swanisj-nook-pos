Social Media Branding Tips

  • Consistency is key: Use your color palette in every post to build brand recognition
  • Photography styling: Your colors pair beautifully with warm woods, cozy textiles, and natural lighting
  • Text hierarchy: Charcoal for headlines, Peat for body text, Battleship Gray for captions
  • Story branding: Use Brandy Rose or Soft Sage circles for highlight covers
  • Seasonal adaptation: Add deeper versions of your colors for winter, lighter tints for summer
  • Gaming aesthetic: Your palette works perfectly with retro gaming vibes and cozy streaming setups
